🔌 BS 3036 Semi-Enclosed Fuse Rule

Must apply a 0.725 correction factor (fusing factor), meaning the conductor's current-carrying capacity (Iz) must be at least:

Iz ≥ In ÷ 0.725  =  In × 1.38

Example: 20 A fuse → Iz must be ≥ 27.6 A
